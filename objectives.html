OBJECTIVES

Program Educational Objectives

PEO Program Educational Objectives
1 Graduates shall be competent professionals skilful in applying technical and managerial skills.
2 Graduates shall have a research orientation to pursue higher research in Computer Science and Engineering domains to complement needs of academia and industry
3 Graduates shall be socially responsible by being champions of green technologies and sustainable engineering practices.

Program Specific Outcomes

PEO Program Educational Objectives
PSO1 Develop mathematical, logical, and programming skills to analyse real world problems and apply them to design computing solutions.
PSO2 Acquire the knowledge of hardware, software, and information systems and use them to foster developments in engineering and scientific domains.
